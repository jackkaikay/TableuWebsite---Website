About the College Data Platform

The college data platform provides access to key information about the sector and individual colleges. The data has been taken from publicly available information sources and the AoC MIDES data sharing service. Charts and tables are updated regularly when new information becomes available.

For more detail on the college data platform or help on using the service. Please contact RCU on 01772 734855 or enquiries@rcu.co.uk
